This repository contains the interactive slides for the seminar **"From LLMs to Agents: Models, Prompts, and Automation"**, presented on **December 23, 2025**, at the **ICAB Incubator** in Brussels.

The presentation provides a technical yet accessible journey from the internal mechanics of Large Language Models (LLMs) to the cutting-edge world of autonomous AI Agents.

## üó∫Ô∏è Presentation Roadmap

1.  **The Mechanics of LLMs:** Probabilistic engines, tokenization, and the Transformer revolution.
2.  **The "Dumb" Side of AI:** Understanding hallucinations, the "Strawberry" problem, and why LLMs fail at basic logic.
3.  **RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation):** Moving from "closed-book" memory to "open-book" factual grounding.
4.  **The Model Landscape:** A deep dive into the LM Arena (GPT-5, Claude 4.5, Llama, and DeepSeek).
5.  **Prompt Engineering:** From "Magic Incantations" to Intentional Design and Chain-of-Thought (CoT).
6.  **Offline AI:** Privacy and local execution using Ollama (Gemma 3, Qwen 2.5).
7.  **Practical Automation:** Generating code, CSVs, Flowcharts (Mermaid), 3D models (OpenSCAD), and Music (ABC notation).
8.  **The Rise of Agents:** Giving the "Brain" a pair of "Hands" via tool-use loops and Multi-Agent Orchestration (CrewAI, LangGraph).
